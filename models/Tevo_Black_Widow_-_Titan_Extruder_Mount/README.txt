# Summary

Simple mount for an E3D Titan Extruder to the Tevo Black Widow 3D printer.

Use M5 screws and M5 lock nuts to mount to Black Widow X carriage.

There are two mounting positions. The higher position (using lower nut recesses) will work for E3D Volcano or other long hot ends. The lower position (using upper nut recesses) will work for E3D V6 or similar length hot ends.

Note: the pictures are from a previous version of the design. After mounting it I realized that the mount would be too high if I had a standard V6 hot end instead of a Volcano.
