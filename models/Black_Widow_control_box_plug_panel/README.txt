# Summary

New backplate for Black Widow controller box, to house female JST-SM plugs in the back of the control box. Allows you to cable the control box more nicely and also easier removal / adding of the cables.

Backplate includes hole for XT60 plug for heat bed, with separate stress relief attachment.

Also included is bracket for 120mm fan, to attach cables with zip tie and prevent them being sliced by the fan blades.

Check screenshot image from slicer for correct orientation for printing without supports.
